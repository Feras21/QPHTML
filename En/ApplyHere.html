​​​​​​​​​​​​​​​​​​​​​


 
Applications for High School Graduates are currently closed.​​
Your interest in the Energy Sector Scholarship Program is highly appreciated.
​
​ Applications Closed​
​
This application will be considered for the Fall 2025 Semester or Term (Starting August/September 2025).

 
The deadline for submitting applications is May 22, 2025.
​
​
By filling the below form, you accept that QatarEnergy stores and processes your data internally, for the purpose of controlling the accuracy of the information, its validity, and its adequacy with the Scholarships program requirements.
QatarEnergy will not share your data with any third party that is not involved in the Scholarships program. 
You have the right of access to your data at any time, through a simple request to QatarEnergy by email at qps@qatarenergy.qa or by calling 40131295 or 40132393.
This right includes the possibility for you to be sent copies of your data, or to have your data updated, or to ask for your data to be removed from QatarEnergy’s database, or to withdraw your permission for the data to be processed by QatarEnergy.​
