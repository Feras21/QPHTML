    ​​​​​​​​​​​​​​​

    ​As marketing agent, Qatar​Energy manages and operates a number of international liaison offices. These offices serve local markets, existing and potential customers in coordinating services activities across products. Local contact information can be found here.​ ​
    ​
    ​

    ​

    QatarEnergy holds the exclusive rights to market, sell and distribute the State of Qatar’s chemical, polymer and fertilizer products. With an international presence in end-markets, QatarEnergy Marketing B.V. is the global marketing and distribution services arm of QatarEnergy. QatarEnergy Marketing B.V. aims to increase QatarEnergy’s global reach and better serve its clients worldwide through a network of offices spread around the world.

     

    VISION

    To enrich our world through the provision of marketing and distribution services for the products entrusted to us.

    MISSION

    We serve our stakeholders and end-users in the global marketplace by adapting to local customer needs.

    We strive to be the most valuable and efficient route to market chemical, polymer and fertilizer products.

    VALUES

    ​
    Integrity

    We are honest with others and ourselves. We meet the highest professional standards in all our business dealings.

    Teamwork

    We embrace world-class employees from different countries, cultures, and backgrounds and with different points of view. We contribute as individuals and succeed in unity by sharing and building on knowledge and opinions that deliver results.

    Sustainability

    We aim to operate our business responsibly, continuing to develop our employees, resources and capabilities, while ensuring our growth the safety of our environment and the prosperity of our societies.

    Innovation

    We are innovative, practical and bold, allowing us to create new ideas and embrace new technologies for the benefit of our customers, our business and our employees. New thinking and new ideas are encouraged and nurtured throughout our global operations.

    Commitment

    On a day to day basis, we aim to lead by example and inspire others. We are reliable by honoring our commitments and delivering results through our dedication and hard work.
